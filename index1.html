Who we are?

AT TGOW, we empower individuals with disabilities to live a life that is independent, free and full of possibility through our products and services.

We advocate for a life that is full of dignity and equality by providing quality disability advocacy information, sharing stories of amazing people who live with a disability and sharing the incredible disability activism work of our able-body allies.

We believe no group should be treated as second class citizens in the 21st century, especially not individuals with disabilities.
