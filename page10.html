Internship and Volunteer Application Form

Traipsin' Global On Wheels' (TGOW) internship application form is now OPEN! Individuals with experience in ANY one (1) of the following areas could apply: Chinese verbal and written proficiency, disability rights, communications, marketing, product design, product development, outreach, web development, social media strategy and search engine optimization (SEO) are especially encouraged to apply. Individuals with the following majors are also welcome to apply: journalism, business management, communications, computer science, and accounting. Please note that this internship is unpaid, but very rewarding. You are able to work remotely from anywhere as long as you have an internet connection.
