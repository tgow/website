Non-Profit Organization

BACKBONES exists to help people with spinal cord injury or disease (SCI/D) and their families connect with their communities. We do that by creating events and experiences that promote awareness and engage people of all abilities.

A full-service accessible travel platform

AbleThrive.com is a platform with content on living life to the fullest with paralysis. Check out articles and videos on life skills, parenting, relationships, activities and travel curated from hundreds of hospitals, organizations, bloggers and companies. We make it easy to find the information you need to thrive.

Tourism for people with disabilities

With their special equipment and the assistance of their staff, Rome & Italy organize tours and excursions for disabled tourists and their families. They know the level of accessibility and difficulties of each location and site that they suggest, having studied and tested it with a staff of specialists.

Learn More!
Rate & Review Service

At iAccess Innovations, we have developed a lifestyle application (iAccess Life) that allows its users to rate and review locations they visit based on their accessibility. We plan to build strategic partnerships with booking agencies and exciting brands to incorporate features that empower our community to seek new adventures, stress-free.

A full-service accessible travel platform

AccessibleGo provides search, reviews, and bookings of accessible hotels, cruises, transport and destinations worldwide. With timely insights, comprehensive resources, and a dynamic traveler community, accessibleGO is quickly becoming the number one travel resource for planning accessible trips.
